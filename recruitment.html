Global Talent Solutions

Globedge Holdings offers comprehensive recruitment and talent acquisition services designed to connect skilled professionals with leading employers across borders. Our specialized team understands the complexities of international recruitment and provides tailored solutions for both employers seeking talent and job seekers looking for opportunities abroad.

For Employers

Cross-Border Hiring

End-to-end international recruitment solutions to help you source talent from global markets, navigate immigration requirements, and facilitate seamless onboarding.

Blue Collar Recruitment

Specialized recruitment services for skilled trades, construction, manufacturing, and service industry positions with thorough skills verification.

White Collar Recruitment

Executive search and professional recruitment for management, technical, and specialized positions across various industries.

Pre-Screening & Verification

Comprehensive candidate screening including background checks, credential verification, skills assessment, and reference validation.

Onboarding Support

Assistance with visa processing, relocation logistics, cultural orientation, and initial settlement support for new international hires.

For Job Seekers

CV Enhancement

Professional CV/resume writing and optimization services tailored to international standards and specific industry requirements.

Targeted Job Matching

Personalized job matching based on your skills, experience, and career goals with direct connections to suitable employers.

LinkedIn Revamp

Complete LinkedIn profile optimization to increase visibility to recruiters and enhance your professional online presence.

Interview Preparation

Comprehensive interview coaching including mock interviews, feedback, and guidance on handling common and challenging questions.

Pre-Departure Support

Assistance with visa applications, document preparation, travel arrangements, and orientation for your new destination.

Industry Specializations

Construction & Engineering

Specialized recruitment for construction managers, civil engineers, architects, MEP specialists, and skilled tradespeople.

Hospitality & Tourism

Talent acquisition for hotels, restaurants, cruise lines, and tourism operations at all levels from entry to executive.

IT & Technology

Recruitment for software developers, IT managers, cybersecurity specialists, and other technology professionals.

Healthcare

Placement services for doctors, nurses, technicians, and healthcare administrators in international settings.
