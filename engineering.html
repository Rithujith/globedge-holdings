MEP Engineering Solutions

Comprehensive mechanical, electrical, and plumbing engineering services

Engineering Excellence for Modern Buildings

Globedge Holdings provides comprehensive MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) engineering solutions that ensure optimal building performance, energy efficiency, and occupant comfort. Our team of experienced engineers combines technical expertise with innovative approaches to deliver sustainable and cost-effective solutions for projects of all sizes.

Smart Design for MEP Systems

HVAC System Design

Custom heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system designs optimized for efficiency, comfort, and indoor air quality.

Electrical System Design

Comprehensive electrical engineering services including power distribution, lighting design, and low voltage systems.

Plumbing System Design

Efficient plumbing system designs including water supply, drainage, and specialized systems for various building types.

HVAC & ESP Calculations

Load Calculations

Precise heating and cooling load calculations to ensure properly sized HVAC equipment for optimal performance and energy efficiency.

Duct & Pipe Sizing

Accurate duct and pipe sizing calculations to ensure proper air and fluid distribution throughout building systems.

ESP & Pressure Drop Analysis

Detailed external static pressure and pressure drop analysis to optimize system performance and equipment selection.

MEP Shop Drawings & Layouts

BIM Coordination

Building Information Modeling (BIM) services for MEP systems to detect clashes, optimize space usage, and improve coordination.

Detailed Shop Drawings

Comprehensive shop drawings with precise dimensions and specifications for fabrication and installation of MEP components.

3D Modeling & Visualization

Advanced 3D modeling and visualization services to help clients understand complex MEP systems before installation.

Quantity Take-Offs & Estimations

Material Quantification

Detailed material take-offs for accurate procurement planning and cost control throughout the project lifecycle.

Cost Estimations

Comprehensive cost estimations for MEP systems to help with budgeting, value engineering, and financial planning.

Value Engineering

Value engineering services to identify cost-saving opportunities without compromising system performance or quality.

Energy Optimization & Compliance

Energy Modeling

Advanced energy modeling and simulation to optimize building performance and reduce operational costs.

Green Building Certification

Support for LEED, WELL, and other green building certification processes with MEP systems that meet sustainability requirements.

Code Compliance

Ensuring all MEP designs comply with local, national, and international building codes and standards.
